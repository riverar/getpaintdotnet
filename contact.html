Contact

General Paint.NET contact email (not for technical support or troubleshooting): (English only please)
For technical support, troubleshooting, questions on how to use Paint.NET, or how to accomplish certain tasks (e.g. drop shadows, reflections), please ask at our forum.
For bug reports, feature requests, suggestions and other feedback, please email us or post in the forum. From within Paint.NET you may conveniently send an e-mail via the Help → Send feedback or bug report... menu item.
If you find a bug, we ask that you include a description of the problem and how to reproduce it. Also, please include a brief description of your system (CPU type and speed, RAM size, version of Windows). If it is a crash bug we also ask that you attach the contents of the "pdncrash.log" file that has been placed on your desktop.
For licensing and distribution inquiries, and for interview or press-related inquiries, please email us at the address given above and ask for Rick Brewster.
